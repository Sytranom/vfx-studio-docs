Boost your productivity and streamline your workflow by mastering the keyboard shortcuts in VFX Studio.

These shortcuts are available globally while the VFX Studio window is focused.

<PropertyReference>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Ctrl', 'S']} />} type="Action">
    Saves the currently selected effect.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Ctrl', 'Z']} />} type="Action">
    Undoes the last action.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Ctrl', 'Y']} />} type="Action">
    Redoes the last undone action.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Ctrl', 'Shift', 'R']} />} type="Action">
    Resimulates the currently selected effect in the viewport, restarting its animation.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Delete']} />} type="Action">
    Deletes the selected emitter or module.
  </Property>
</PropertyReference>

Quickly navigate and toggle the different panels within the UI.

<PropertyReference>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['F1']} />} type="Toggle">
    Toggles the Emitter Properties panel.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['F2']} />} type="Toggle">
    Toggles the Sliders panel.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['F3']} />} type="Toggle">
    Toggles the Curve Editor panel.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['F4']} />} type="Toggle">
    Toggles the Advanced Panel.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Tab']} />} type="Action">
    Cycles focus between the currently open panels.
  </Property>
</PropertyReference>

Shortcuts for interacting with effects directly in the 3D viewport.

<PropertyReference>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['F']} />} type="Action">
    Frames the selected effect, centering it in the camera's view.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Alt', 'LMB Drag']} />} type="Action">
    Orbits the camera around the effect's origin.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Alt', 'RMB Drag']} />} type="Action">
    Dollies (zooms) the camera in and out.
  </Property>
  <Property name={<Keybind keys={['Ctrl', 'Alt', 'Click']} />} type="Action">
    Selects the emitter source of a particle you click on.
  </Property>
</PropertyReference>

<Callout type="tip" title="Customizable Keybinds">
  All keybinds can be customized in the Settings panel. You can even <Highlight>create and export</Highlight> your own keymap presets to share with your team.
</Callout>
