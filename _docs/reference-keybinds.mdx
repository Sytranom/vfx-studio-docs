Boost your productivity and streamline your workflow by mastering the keyboard shortcuts in VFX Studio.

These shortcuts are available globally while the VFX Studio window is focused.

- **Ctrl + S**: Save the currently selected effect.
- **Ctrl + Z**: Undo the last action.
- **Ctrl + Y**: Redo the last undone action.
- **Ctrl + Shift + R**: Resimulate the currently selected effect in the viewport, restarting its animation.
- **Delete**: Delete the selected emitter or module.

Quickly navigate and toggle the different panels within the UI.

- **F1**: Toggle the Emitter Properties panel.
- **F2**: Toggle the Sliders panel.
- **F3**: Toggle the Curve Editor panel.
- **F4**: Toggle the Advanced Panel.
- **Tab**: Cycle focus between the currently open panels.

Shortcuts for interacting with effects directly in the 3D viewport.

- **F**: Frame the selected effect, centering it in the camera's view.
- **Alt + Left Click + Drag**: Orbit the camera around the effect's origin.
- **Alt + Right Click + Drag**: Dolly (zoom) the camera in and out.
- **Ctrl + Alt + Click**: Select the emitter source of a particle you click on.

<Callout type="tip" title="Customizable Keybinds">
  All keybinds can be customized in the Settings panel. You can even create and export your own keymap presets to share with your team.
</Callout>
